📜 Terms of Privacy · The Acorn Compact

At Acorn Recipe Diary, we treat your data like a handwritten recipe — with respect, care, and never a trace of artificiality. This "Terms of Privacy" document merges our spirit of transparency (grey integrity) and warmth (yellow accountability). It explains how we gather, use, and protect any information when you visit our grey + yellow kitchen, subscribe to our zine, or participate in our community suppers. The principles here mirror our cooking philosophy: honest ingredients, minimal processing, and absolute devotion to your trust.

⚖️ Last updated: May 5, 2026 · Effective for all our seasonal editions.

🌾 1. What We Gather · The Grey Harvest

Just as we forage wild acorns with intention, we collect only the essential data needed to enhance your experience and serve our handwritten community. The types of information we may collect include:

  • 🍂 Personal identifiers (yellow notes): When you subscribe to our newsletter, request an Acorn Pledge card, or join a Grey + Yellow Supper Club, we may ask for your name, email address, and optionally postal address (for physical zines).
  • 📖 Usage data (grey echoes): Standard server logs, anonymized page visits, and aggregated interactions with our recipe index — never sold, only used to improve our storytelling and recipe flow.
  • 🍋 Correspondence & stories: If you send us a handwritten letter, postcard, or email hello@acornrecipediary.com, we retain your message to respond and occasionally share anonymous excerpts (with your consent).
  • 🍯 Cookies & similar technologies: We use only essential or functional cookies to remember your preferences (e.g., dark mode toggle if added). Our Cookies Policy (linked above) is written in simple, golden-hued language.

We never collect sensitive data like payment details (we use secure third-party processors for zine purchases), nor do we scrape user data. The Acorn Pledge ensures we follow the "Grey Minimalism" principle: less data, more soul.

💛 2. How We Use Your Information · Golden Purpose

Every grain of data we collect serves a clear, warm purpose — like turmeric brightening a broth. We use information to:

  • ✨ Send you seasonal recipe digests, event invitations (Acorn Ceremony, supper clubs), and handwritten-style newsletters (you can unsubscribe anytime with one click).
  • 📝 Respond to your queries, pledges, or recipe suggestions — we aim to reply within 48 hours, often with a personal story from our grey notebook.
  • 📊 Improve site navigation and understand which recipes resonate most with our community (e.g., “Golden Lentil Stew” vs. “Smoky Grey Broth”).
  • 🧾 Fulfill orders for our printed zine series — we share your name and address only with our ethical print partner, under strict confidentiality.
  • 🌰 Maintain the integrity of our Acorn Pledge Registry (over 2,347 handwritten pledges stored in a locked grey archive).

We will never use your information for AI training, targeted ads, or any form of automated profiling. Our diary is human-first, always.

🛡️ 3. Sharing & Transparency · The Community Table

Respecting your privacy is as sacred as respecting a family recipe. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Limited sharing occurs only in these specific, necessary scenarios:

  • ⚖️ Legal obligations: If required by law (e.g., to comply with a subpoena or protect our rights), we may disclose data — but we will notify you unless prohibited.
  • 📦 Service providers: We use trusted third-party tools for email newsletters (MailerLite) and secure payment processing (Stripe). They are bound by data protection agreements and cannot use your data for any other purpose.
  • 🍂 Community testimonials: Only with explicit permission, we may publish your first name and your grey-yellow story on our site (e.g., “— Sam from Vermont”).

Our servers are located in the USA, and we comply with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA principles for EU and California residents (see Section 7).

🔐 4. Data Security · Cast Iron Protection

We protect your information like a cast iron skillet — seasoned, sturdy, and resilient. We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards:

  • ✅ Encryption (SSL/TLS) across our website.
  • ✅ Limited access to personal data — only core team members (Margot, Samir, Eleanor) have access, and they’ve each signed the Acorn Confidentiality Pledge.
  • ✅ Regular security audits and automatic backups encrypted.
  • ✅ Offline storage: physical pledge letters are kept in a locked fireproof cabinet at our Portland studio.

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours via email and post an update on our homepage. Your safety is our golden rule.

🌍 5. Your Rights · The Acorn Control Panel

You retain full ownership of your data, just as a cook owns their signature dish. Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • 🔍 Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • ✏️ Rectify inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • 🗑️ Request deletion of your data (subject to legal or archival exceptions like the Acorn Pledge registry if you want to revoke — we will honor it).
  • 📤 Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a structured format.
  • 🚫 Opt-out of marketing communications at any time via unsubscribe link or email.

To exercise any right, please contact our Privacy Keeper at privacy@acornrecipediary.com or mail us at 42 Hazel Lane, Portland, OR 97204. We will respond within 30 days, often quicker. No automated chatbots — just a real human reading your request by a grey window.

🍪 6. Cookies & Tracking · As Simple as Butter Cookies

Our approach to cookies is minimalist — like shortbread with a hint of lemon. We only use:

  • Essential cookies: To enable basic site functions (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences, navigation).
  • Preference cookies: To store display settings such as font size if you prefer larger text.
  • Analytics (anonymized): We use a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics tool (Fathom or self-hosted) to count page views without tracking individual users. No tracking across the web, no Google Analytics.

You can manage cookie settings via your browser. Blocking essential cookies may affect functionality. For a full breakdown, view our separate Cookies Policy — another human-written, jargon-free page.

🧑‍🍳 7. International Users & Legal Bases

Acorn Recipe Diary is based in the United States, but our community spans from Oregon to Oslo. If you are visiting from the European Economic Area, UK, or other regions with comprehensive data laws, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: For newsletters, pledge submissions, and optional data sharing.
  • Contractual necessity: To fulfill zine orders or event registrations.
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our site, prevent fraud, and maintain the diary’s security — always balanced with your rights.

Cross-border data transfers are safeguarded via Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent protections. California residents: You may request information about any “sale” of data (we do not sell) and request opt-out from sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising — which we do not engage in whatsoever. For CCPA requests, email privacy@acornrecipediary.com with “California Rights” in the subject line.

📅 8. Children’s Privacy · Little Foragers

Our content is intended for general audiences, including families who cook together. However, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data without parental consent, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it. Any child-focused recipes are designed for adult supervision — our grey + yellow kitchen welcomes all ages but with guardian guidance.

🖋️ 9. Changes To These Terms of Privacy

As our diary evolves (new recipes, seasonal projects), these terms may be updated to reflect new practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes via:

  • A clear banner on the homepage for at least 30 days; and
  • If you are a newsletter subscriber, a short, readable summary in our next edition (no legalese, just clear notes).

The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version. Your continued use after changes means you accept the new terms – but we will never retroactively reduce your rights.

🌟 The Acorn Promise: We will never sell your data, never use dark patterns, and never betray the grey & yellow trust. Every byte of information is treated like a handwritten recipe passed down through generations — with honor.
